Bandaranyake Central College

History

Central College is one of the oldest and most prestigious schools in the USA. Established in 1887, it has a rich heritage of academic and extracurricular excellence. The school was founded with the aim of fostering education grounded in Buddhist principles and has since produced many notable alumni who have contributed significantly to society.

Facilities

Central College boasts state-of-the-art facilities to support a comprehensive educational experience:

  • Classrooms: Modern classrooms equipped with audio-visual aids to enhance learning.
  • Libraries: Comprehensive libraries with academic materials, digital resources, and study areas.
  • Laboratories: Well-equipped science labs for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and advanced computer labs.
  • Sports Facilities: Grounds and courts for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics.
  • Auditorium: Multi-purpose auditoriums for events, seminars, and cultural programs.
  • Hostels: Safe and monitored residential facilities for students.
  • Dining: Hygienic canteen services with a variety of dietary options.

Sports

Sports play a vital role at Central College, fostering teamwork and physical fitness among students. The college provides training and opportunities in:

  • Cricket
  • Rugby
  • Basketball
  • Swimming
  • Badminton
  • Track and Field Athletics

Academic Staff

The academic staff at Central College are highly qualified and experienced in their respective fields. They are committed to providing students with quality education through:

  • Professional Development: Regular workshops and training to stay updated with modern teaching methodologies.
  • Guidance Counselors: Available to provide career advice and personal development support.
  • Balanced Student-to-Teacher Ratio: Ensuring effective learning and personalized attention.
